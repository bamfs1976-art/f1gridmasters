HOW TO PLAY
🎲Random draft order — pick positions are randomised at the start of the season
🔄Rotating draft order — Race 1 is randomised. Each race, the previous first pick moves to last. Race 2, second to last. After 16 races every player has held every position exactly once
👤Select your identity — tap your name on the Draft page so the app knows who you are
🥇Rank as many drivers as you like — tap drivers to build your preference list in order. The more you rank, the safer your pick
🔒Submit any time — lock in your picks whenever you are ready, in any order. No waiting for others
⚡Auto-resolved — once everyone submits, draft position decides priority. P1 gets their top choice first, then P2, and so on
🚫No duplicates — if your 1st choice is taken, you drop to your next preference. Ranking more drivers means a better safety net
🏁Real results entered after each Grand Prix — points awarded instantly
🏆Most points after all 22 races wins the championship!
